About the project

CipherSafe was developed as a college group project at BCIT.

It aims to help people who are interested in upgrading their online security to make sure that our private online accounts, which we use for everything in the modern world are protected from malicious actors. We accomplish this by safekeeping user's passwords, in a secure manner, and by educating users on safe online behaviour and data breaches.
